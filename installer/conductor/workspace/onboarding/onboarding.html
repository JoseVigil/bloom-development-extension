Identity — Architecture
How identity works
in BTIPS.
BTIPS does not create accounts or store credentials. Identity is established through a master Chromium profile where you register trusted accounts — Google, Gemini, and GitHub.

These accounts are secured by the browser vault and hardware encryption on your device. BTIPS simply detects the existing identity layer and uses it.

The Discovery page runs inside your browser profile and guides you through registering each account. Once all three are confirmed, your sovereign identity is anchored to this machine.
